# GENERAL INFORMATION

**Question**

Can you briefly describe your current role, main responsibilities, and the types of products you've worked on?

**What this assesses**

- Career scope and relevance
- Product and domain exposure

**Expected answer (in general)**

The candidate summarizes their role, seniority, team size, product type (B2B/B2C), and level of ownership without going into excessive detail.

---

# LEADERSHIP EXPERIENCE

**Question**

What leadership experience do you have, and how have you led or mentored others?

**What this assesses**

- People leadership vs. individual contribution
- Coaching and decision-making skills

**Expected answer (in general)**

A strong candidate describes mentoring, code/design reviews, how many people they led, decision ownership, conflict resolution, and enabling team performance rather than just holding a title.

---

# PRODUCT ANALYTICS EXPERIENCE

**Question**

What experience do you have with product analytics, and how have you used data to inform decisions?

**What this assesses**

- Data-driven mindset
- Product impact awareness

**Expected answer (in general)**

The candidate references metrics such as DAU, retention, funnel conversion, or performance metrics, and explains how data influenced product or technical decisions.

# MOBILE DEVELOPMENT

# REACTIVE APPLICATIONS

## Experience & Architecture

**Questions**

- What experience do you have building reactive or real-time applications?
- What technologies did you use (e.g., WebSocket, SSE)?

**What this assesses**

- Real-time system design
- Event-based thinking

**Expected answer (in general)**

The candidate explains use cases such as chat, live dashboards, or notifications, and why specific real-time technologies were chosen.

## Data Consistency

**Question**

How did you handle data conflicts in reactive systems, and what solutions did you implement?

**What this assesses**

- Distributed systems understanding
- Problem-solving under concurrency

**Expected answer (in general)**

Mentions versioning, timestamps, reconciliation logic, idempotency, or eventual consistency strategies.

## Alternatives

**Question**

Are there other technologies or approaches you've used to build reactive applications?

**Expected answer (in general)**

The candidate shows breadth without over-claiming (e.g., message queues, polling, streaming).
