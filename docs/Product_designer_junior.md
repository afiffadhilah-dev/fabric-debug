1. Design Process 

Question 
Can you walk us through your design process from concept to final delivery? 

What this question assesses 

Design thinking and structure 

Ability to work end-to-end 

Clarity of communication 

Expected answer (in general) 
A clear, structured process that typically includes understanding the problem and business goals, conducting user research, defining requirements, ideation and exploration, wireframing, visual design, prototyping, validation, iteration, and final handoff.  

2. User Research 

Question 
How do you approach user research? 

What this question assesses 

User-centric mindset 

Research literacy (not just UI skills) 

Expected answer (in general) 
The candidate explains how they identify research goals, choose appropriate methods (interviews, surveys, usability testing, data analysis, etc), and synthesize insights into actionable findings such as personas, journey maps, or problem statements.
