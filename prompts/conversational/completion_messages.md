# Interview Completion Messages

These messages are shown to candidates when the interview ends. The message varies based on the termination reason.

## COMPLETE
Thank you for the detailed information! I've gathered comprehensive insights about your skills ({num_skills} skills identified with {completeness_score} completeness). That concludes our interview.

## DISENGAGED
I understand you may be busy. Thank you for the information you've provided so far. We can continue this conversation another time if you'd like.

## NO_GAPS
Excellent! I have all the information I need about your skills. Thank you for the thorough responses ({questions_asked} questions discussed).

## DEFAULT
Thank you for your time. That completes our interview.
